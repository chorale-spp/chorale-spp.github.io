Our Story

About the Ensemble

We are a community of voices united by a shared love of choral music — from timeless sacred works to vibrant contemporary arrangements. Founded in 2022 under the patronage of Saint Padre Pio, our chorale has grown into a family bound not only by song, but by faith, purpose, and fellowship.

This portal is our shared space — a place to stay connected, access our repertoire, celebrate our moments together, and deepen the craft we hold dear.

Every voice matters. Every voice belongs.

Passion
Music is not a task — it is a calling we answer together.
Community
We are stronger, richer, and more resonant as one.
Excellence
We pursue beauty with diligence, humility, and joy.
Faith
Our voices rise as one offering of praise and devotion.
